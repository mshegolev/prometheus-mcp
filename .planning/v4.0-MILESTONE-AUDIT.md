## Verdict: ⚠️ CONDITIONAL — shipped with documented gaps

The milestone was marked complete (6/6 phases) on 2026-06-18 and released, but
did **not** pass through GSD's formal verification gate, and two of its four
feature areas shipped as **simulation** rather than real implementations. The
correlation and (post-fix) root-cause features are genuinely delivered; the
dependency-mapping and trend-analysis features are not.

## Process gaps

- **No VERIFICATION.md files exist for any phase.** Phases 8–13 have SUMMARY.md
  but were never formally verified during execute-phase. Per GSD this is a
  blocker ("unverified phase"). The absence explains why real defects survived
  "completion" and were only found/fixed post-release this session:
  - v0.4.1 — the four v4 correlation/federation tools were never registered on
    the server (`server.py` didn't import their modules).
  - v0.4.2 — a registry lazy-init regression broke 79 tests; the lint gate was
    red. Both fixed; CI green.
  - v0.4.3 — RCA change-point detection fabricated events; now honest-empty.
    `dependency`, `trend_analysis`, `tools_federation_v4` flagged EXPERIMENTAL.

released** (COR + reachable RCA). The **DEP/TRE requirements are not genuinely
met** — they shipped as simulation and are flagged EXPERIMENTAL/unreachable.

Two honest closure paths:
- **Archive v4.0 with documented gaps** — accept DEP/TRE/RCA-03 as deferred debt
  (recorded here and in STATE.md), then open a follow-up milestone to make them
  real. Reflects reality: correlation shipped; dependency/trend are stubs.
- **Do not archive as "fully done"** — open the follow-up milestone first and
  complete DEP/TRE/RCA-03 before closing v4.0.
